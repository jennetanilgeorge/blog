Why Paris?

Paris is a city of timeless beauty, rich culture, and romantic charm. With its breathtaking architecture, world-class art, and quaint cafes, it has always captured my heart.

Top 3 Places I Want to Visit

  • Eiffel Tower: An iconic symbol of France. I want to visit at night when it's glittering with lights!



  • Louvre Museum: To admire the Mona Lisa and get immersed in world-class art and history.



  • Seine River: A romantic sunset cruise on the Seine River to see the magic of Paris from the water.

Dream Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Paris, check in, visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy coffee at a nearby Parisian café.
Day 2: Explore the Louvre Museum, admire the artwork, then walk through Montmartre's artistic streets.
Day 3: Take a Seine River cruise, shop at Champs-Élysées, and watch the sunset from a viewpoint.

Fun Facts About Paris

  • The Eiffel Tower can be 15 cm taller in summer due to metal expansion.
  • There are over 1,800 bakeries in Paris.
  • Paris was originally a Roman city called “Lutetia.”
  • It is known as “The City of Light” because it was one of the first to adopt street lighting.
